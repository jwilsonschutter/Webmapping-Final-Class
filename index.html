Welcome!

This site is meant to provide a basic understanding of how the subway impacts a variety of factors in NYC, ranging from car ownership, to income, and even density! In order to use the website please click on the buttons labeled Cars, Income, and Density to your right. You can also hover over any zip code or area in order to view more information about that specific zip code, you can also examine specific subway stations by zooming in further as they don't appear unless you're close. This map was made in order to examine how NYC's zip codes compare to their MTA access, along three different variables, those being cars per person, household income, and the density of the area. The map utilizes MTA lines and stops which are visible on the map should you zoom in.
